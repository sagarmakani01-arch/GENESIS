# Overview

Starting a company is one of the most challenging endeavors. Founders often lack access to experienced mentors, technical expertise, market insights, financial guidance, or strategic planning during the early stages.

GENESIS is designed to bridge that gap.

Rather than functioning as a generic AI chatbot, GENESIS acts as an intelligent co-founder capable of assisting entrepreneurs across every stage of building a company—from validating an idea to launching and scaling a business.

It combines research, strategic thinking, business intelligence, and AI reasoning into a unified platform built to support founders in making better decisions faster.

---

# The Problem

Building a startup requires expertise across multiple domains.

A founder must simultaneously become:

- Product Manager
- Market Researcher
- Business Strategist
- Financial Planner
- Technical Architect
- Growth Marketer
- Sales Executive
- Investor Pitch Creator

Most early-stage founders lack the resources to hire specialists for every role, leading to costly mistakes, slow execution, and failed ventures.

---

# The Solution

GENESIS functions as an AI Co-Founder.

Instead of providing isolated answers, it collaborates with founders throughout the startup lifecycle, offering strategic recommendations, business insights, structured planning, and execution support.

Its purpose is to reduce uncertainty, improve decision-making, and accelerate startup development.

---

# Core Features

## Startup Idea Validation

Analyze business ideas and identify strengths, weaknesses, risks, and opportunities before investing significant time or capital.

---

## Market Research

Research industries, competitors, customer pain points, market trends, and potential opportunities.

---

## Business Strategy

Develop scalable business models, pricing strategies, competitive positioning, and long-term growth plans.

---

## Product Planning

Help define MVP scope, product roadmaps, feature prioritization, and development strategies.

---

## Technical Guidance

Recommend suitable technology stacks, software architectures, and implementation strategies based on project requirements.

---

## Financial Planning

Assist with budgeting, revenue models, pricing, runway estimation, and financial forecasting.

---

## Pitch Preparation

Generate investor-ready pitch structures, executive summaries, business plans, and presentation content.

---

## Growth Strategy

Develop customer acquisition plans, marketing campaigns, branding strategies, and go-to-market execution.

---

## Decision Support

Evaluate trade-offs, compare alternatives, assess risks, and support founders in making strategic business decisions.

---

# How It Works

1. Share your startup idea.
2. GENESIS analyzes your business concept.
3. It identifies opportunities and potential challenges.
4. Strategic recommendations are generated.
5. Build product roadmaps and execution plans.
6. Continue refining your startup as it evolves.

# Installation

Clone the repository

```bash
git clone https://github.com/sagarmakani01-arch/GENESIS.git
```

Navigate into the project

```bash
cd GENESIS
```

Install dependencies

```bash
npm install
```

Run the development server

```bash
npm run dev
```
